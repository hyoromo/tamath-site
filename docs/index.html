One Stroke Calculation Brain Training Game

This game is a puzzle game where you calculate addition, subtraction and multiplication in one continuous stroke.
The controls are simple! Just connect the numbers displayed on the screen in one line.
The rules are simple so anyone can play easily, but as you level up the difficulty increases.
Work your brain hard and aim for a high score!

3 Difficulty Levels

  • 【EASY】
    Just simple addition and subtraction. Recommended for beginners.
  • 【NORMAL】
    Multiplication questions are added.
  • 【HARD】
    In addition to addition, subtraction and multiplication, the number of digits also increases.

Ranking

There is also a ranking system to compete with top players around the world.
Update your records daily and aim to become the top player!
